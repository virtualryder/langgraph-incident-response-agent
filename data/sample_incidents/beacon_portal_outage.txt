INCIDENT REPORT — INITIAL FILING

Incident ID: INC-2025-0847
System: BEACON Benefits Portal (Bay State Department of Benefits)
Reported By: Sarah Chen, On-Call SRE
Detection Time: 2025-05-06T09:09:00
Filing Time: 2025-05-06T09:17:00

INCIDENT DESCRIPTION:
BEACON citizen benefits portal is returning HTTP 503 Service Unavailable errors
to all users attempting to file weekly unemployment claims. This began at
approximately 9:09 AM during the Monday morning peak filing window.

Symptoms observed:
- 100% of POST /api/v2/claims/weekly requests returning 503
- PagerDuty alert triggered at 9:13 AM from CloudWatch alarm BEACON-App-ErrorRate-Critical
- Nginx access logs showing upstream connect failures
- Application appears to be running (pods healthy in ECS) but not serving traffic

Citizens impacted: Monday is the highest filing day — approximately 47,000 weekly
unemployment claimants file on Monday mornings. Weekly benefit disbursement
of ~$3.2M is at risk if filing window closes without resolution.

SNAP eligibility check API also reporting degraded performance (elevated latency,
some timeouts) — may be related or separate issue.

No scheduled maintenance window was active. Last deployment was approximately
36 hours ago (Friday afternoon).

Initial investigation: Application logs show database-related errors but full
root cause not yet determined. No evidence of external attack at this time.
Need full investigation to confirm root cause and determine if citizen PII
was exposed in any error conditions.
